Meet Our Master Patissier

Chef Marie Laurent

With over 20 years of experience in the world's finest kitchens, Chef Laurent brings an unparalleled passion for pastry arts to Éclat.

Trained in Paris and having worked alongside Michelin-starred chefs, her creations are a harmonious blend of traditional techniques and innovative flair.

"Dessert isn't just the final course—it's the crescendo of the dining experience, a moment of pure joy that should linger in memory long after the last bite."
